Our Workflow

At Iskolutions, we streamline every step—from planning to deployment—ensuring efficiency, collaboration, and high-quality solutions through innovation and best practices.

Consultation

This is where we discuss your goals, requirements, and preferences, allowing us to understand your vision and provide informed consultation. Through open communication, we collaborate on decisions, negotiate terms, and craft tailored solutions for you.

Agreement

Once we agree on the details of the project (i.e., schedule, budget, scope), we will create a document formalizing our commitment and detailed agreement to set clear expectations.

Design

We'll design the user interface, integrating your brand elements to create a visually engaging and user-friendly experience.

Prototyping

I'll develop a visual prototype to give you an early preview of the layout and functionality, ensuring we refine the design before moving into full development.

Revisions

Your feedback is essential in this phase as we refine the design. We make necessary adjustments to ensure it aligns with your vision and expectations.

Launch

After rigorous testing to ensure smooth performance, we launch your website, making it live and ready to support your online goals.
